Why Join Us?

Membership means becoming part of a 70-year legacy.

By joining, you:

  • Connect with a proud and welcoming cultural community
  • Provide children with opportunities to learn and lead
  • Support seniors and intergenerational initiatives
  • Help sustain cultural programs for future generations
  • Contribute to shaping Aristotelis' future direction
  • Priority invitations to club events and celebrations
  • Special offers on products or services from our major sponsors

For further information about our membership, please contact info.ccofa@gmail.com

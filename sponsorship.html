Sponsorship

Partner With Us

For seven decades, Aristotelis has been sustained by volunteer dedication and community generosity.

Sponsorship enables us to:

  • Maintain affordable youth participation
  • Deliver inclusive cultural festivals and events
  • Support seniors programming
  • Invest in costumes, equipment and facilities
  • Expand leadership opportunities for young people

We proudly partner with businesses who value culture, community and continuity.

For further information about our sponsorship packages, please contact info.ccofa@gmail.com

Community Partners and Sponsors

We proudly acknowledge the support of Goodyear Autocare Epping, our 2026 Platinum Sponsor and BASE, Plenty Valley, our 2026 Gold Sponsor. Their generous support helps us continue celebrating 70 years of culture, community and tradition while supporting our youth programs and community initiatives. We sincerely thank them for investing in the future of our cultural community.

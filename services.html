Youth Group

Our Youth Group brings young people together to connect, build friendships and celebrate their Macedonian heritage in a welcoming and inclusive environment. Through cultural activities, social events, games nights, outings and performances, we create opportunities for young people to develop confidence, leadership and a strong sense of belonging.

Our Youth Group aims to:

  • Build lasting friendships and social connections
  • Strengthen cultural identity and pride
  • Encourage leadership, teamwork and peer mentoring
  • Provide positive, inclusive experiences beyond school
  • Support wellbeing through creativity, culture and community

Whether they're performing on stage, attending a social event or helping organise community activities, our young members are the next generation of cultural ambassadors and leaders. By encouraging youth voice and participation, we ensure our programs are shaped with young people, creating experiences that are engaging, meaningful and fun.

Senior Citizens Club

Our Senior Citizens Club provides a culturally familiar and socially supportive space for older Florinians to gather and connect.
